Singapore Polytechnic's Sustainability Efforts
SP launched Singapore's first solar photovoltaics recycling line

Singapore Polytechnic (SP) launched the first solar photovoltaics recycling line, which aims to recycle the components of solar panels, including materials like silver and glass. The system incorporates a machine that disassembles the panels. In addition, SP has established two laboratories, namely the SP-Sembcorp Solar PV Research Lab and SP-CHINT Smart Electrical Power Training Laboratory, to further advance research and development in the solar energy field.
